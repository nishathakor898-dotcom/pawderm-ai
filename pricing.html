FAQ

Pricing FAQs

Is PawDerm AI a diagnosis tool?

No. PawDerm lists possible categories and red flags so owners and clinics can communicate more clearly.

Can clinics use this before appointments?

Yes. The clinic version is meant for structured pre-visit intake, not diagnosis automation.

Do these plans include real veterinary care?

No. These plans cover the software. Veterinary diagnosis, treatment, and prescribing remain with licensed professionals.
